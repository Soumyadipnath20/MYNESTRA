Menstrual Products

1. Sanitary Pads

Sanitary pads are absorbent materials worn externally and are available in different sizes and absorbencies.
How to Use
Place the pad on your underwear, with the adhesive side facing down. Change every 4-6 hours, or more often if necessary. Dispose of used pads in a sanitary way.

2. Tampons

Tampons are inserted into the vagina to absorb menstrual flow. They come with different applicators and absorbencies.
How to Use
Insert the tampon into the vaginal canal using your fingers or an applicator. Change every 4-8 hours. Never leave a tampon in for more than 8 hours.

3. Menstrual Cups

Menstrual cups are flexible silicone cups that collect period fluid rather than absorbing it.
How to Use Fold the cup, then insert it into the vagina. Once inside, it unfolds to create a seal. Empty and rinse every 12 hours.

4. Period Panties

Period panties are reusable absorbent underwear designed for different flow levels.
How to UseWear like regular underwear. It absorbs menstrual flow and can be worn alone or as backup protection. Wash after use.
For better demonstration click here.

5. Menstrual Discs

Menstrual discs are flexible, reusable or disposable discs that sit at the base of the vaginal canal, collecting period fluid while allowing for mess-free intimacy.
How to Use Fold the disc and insert it into the vagina, positioning it near the cervix. It collects menstrual fluid. Remove after 12 hours, rinse, and reuse.

6. Reusable Cloth Pads

Reusable cloth pads are eco-friendly, washable menstrual products made from absorbent fabric, offering comfort and sustainability.
How to Use Place on underwear, with the absorbent side facing up. Change every 4-6 hours. Wash after each use.
For better demonstration click here.

7. Menstrual Sponges

Menstrual sponges are natural or synthetic absorbent materials inserted into the vagina to collect period fluid, offering a soft and reusable alternative.
How to Use Wet the sponge, insert it into the vagina, and position it to cover the cervix. Remove and rinse after 4-6 hours. Can be reused.
